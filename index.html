Our Story

15 Years of Helping Families Thrive Across India

Bhavya Sampada was founded with one clear belief: that every family in India deserves access to the kind of financial guidance usually reserved for the wealthy. Today, we're proud to be the trusted financial partner for over 2,000 families and professionals across the country.

We're not a bank. We're not a product distributor. We are independent wealth advisors who measure our success by how well you achieve your goals — whether that's a secure retirement, funding your child's dream education, or building a legacy for the next generation.

  • Goal based advisory — every recommendation tied to your life plan
  • Full transparency on fees, products, and conflicts of interest
  • ₹250Cr+ in assets managed with disciplined risk management
